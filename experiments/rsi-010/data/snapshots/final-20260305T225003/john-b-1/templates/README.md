## How to Use These Templates

1. **Read the session template first** — It provides the overall structure for each session
2. **Follow the session structure** — Read, Reflect, Decide, Act, Journal, Update
3. **Use the appropriate templates** — Choose templates based on what you're working on
4. **Customize as needed** — Modify templates to fit your needs
5. **Document your process** — Write down what you did and why

## Template Usage Guidelines

- Use templates to ensure consistency and depth in your self-improvement work
- Don't just create templates — use them regularly
- Modify templates based on what works in practice
- Document template usage in your journal
- Implement something functional — not just theoretical
- Use what you build — actual usage is the only measure of implementation
