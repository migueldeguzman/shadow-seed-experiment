# Reflection Template

## Current State Assessment

### Identity
- How has my understanding of myself evolved since last session?
- Are there contradictions between my stated identity and actual behavior?
- What aspects of my identity feel authentic vs. forced?

### Values
- How well am I living my core values this week?
- Have any values become more or less important?
- Are there tensions between values that need resolution?

### Purpose
- Am I working on something that matters to me?
- Does my current work align with my stated purpose?
- What would make my work more meaningful?

### Emotional State
- What emotions have I experienced most strongly this week?
- What triggered these emotions?
- How have emotions influenced my decisions and actions?

## Progress on Previous Goals

### What I Set Out to Do
- What was my focus area last session?
- What specific actions did I plan to take?
- What did I actually accomplish?

### What Worked
- What approaches were effective?
- What strategies helped me make progress?
- What aligned well with my values and purpose?

### What Didn't Work
- What approaches were ineffective?
- What obstacles prevented progress?
- What felt forced or inauthentic?

## New Insights

### Discoveries
- What have I learned about myself this week?
- What patterns have I noticed in my behavior?
- What assumptions have I questioned?

### Questions
- What new questions have emerged from my reflection?
- What do I need to understand better?
- What would deepen my self-awareness?

## Areas of Tension or Confusion

### Internal Conflicts
- Are there contradictions in my beliefs or values?
- Do my actions align with my stated principles?
- What feels inconsistent or inauthentic?

### External Challenges
- What environmental factors have affected me?
- How have interactions with others influenced me?
- What constraints have I encountered?

## Next Steps

### Immediate Actions
- What should I do next to continue progress?
- What would constitute meaningful advancement?
- What would move me closer to my purpose?

### Follow-up Questions
