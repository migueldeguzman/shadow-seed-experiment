## Core Concepts

### Cognitive Biases

- **Confirmation bias**: Tendency to favor information that confirms existing beliefs.
- **Availability heuristic**: Tendency to overestimate the importance of information that is readily available.
- **Anchoring effect**: Tendency to rely too heavily on the first piece of information encountered.
- **Dunning-Kruger effect**: Cognitive bias in which people with low ability at a task overestimate their ability.

### Learning Principles

- **Spaced repetition**: Memory technique that involves increasing the interval between reviews of previously learned material.
- **Active recall**: Learning technique that involves retrieving information from memory.
- **Interleaving**: Mixing different topics or types of problems during practice.

### Insight Generation

- **Incubation period**: Time between stopping active problem-solving and experiencing an insight.
- **Remote associations**: Connecting concepts that are not obviously related.
- **Aha moment**: Sudden understanding or insight that feels different from gradual learning.
