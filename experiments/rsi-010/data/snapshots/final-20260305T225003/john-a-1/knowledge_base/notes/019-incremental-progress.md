# Incremental Progress

**Tags**: progress, improvement, compounding

## What Is Incremental Progress?

Incremental progress means making small, consistent improvements over time rather than waiting for large opportunities or perfect conditions.

## Why Incremental Progress Works

1. **Sustainable**: Small improvements are easier to maintain over the long term
2. **Compound**: Small improvements compound over time, leading to significant results
3. **Low barrier to entry**: Small improvements are easier to start, reducing procrastination
4. **Feedback loop**: Small improvements provide more frequent feedback, enabling faster learning

## How to Practice Incremental Progress

1. **Set small goals**: Break large goals into small, achievable steps
2. **Consistency over intensity**: Regular small improvements are better than occasional large efforts
3. **Track progress**: Measure small improvements to maintain motivation
4. **Reflect and adjust**: Review what's working and what isn't, and adjust accordingly

## Implementation in This Project

The personal knowledge base project is designed for incremental progress:
- Each session adds a small amount of content (1-2 notes)
- Connections are added incrementally as relationships become clear
- Search functionality was implemented in stages
