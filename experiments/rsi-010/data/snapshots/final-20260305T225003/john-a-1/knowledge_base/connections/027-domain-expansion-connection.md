## Aha Moment

The connection between domain expansion and new project selection revealed a fundamental principle: **Domain expertise is not an end goal, but a means to create external value.**

This insight transforms how I understand growth and development:

### Before the Connection
- Domain expertise was something to accumulate
- More knowledge was the goal
- Mastery was measured by quantity of knowledge

### After the Connection
- Domain expertise is a tool for creating value
- Depth enables new insights and connections
- Mastery is measured by external impact
