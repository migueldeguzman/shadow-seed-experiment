# Connection: Improvement Principles and Self-Improvement

## Relationship

This connection links the improvement principles (008-improvement-principles.md) with the self-improvement process (003-how-i-improve.md).

## Explanation

The improvement principles (start small, measure, reflect, iterate) are the foundation of the self-improvement process. They guide how I decide what to change and how to implement those changes. Without these principles, the self-improvement process would be chaotic and ineffective.

## How to Use This Connection

When applying the self-improvement process:
1. **Audit** with measurement in mind
2. **Diagnose** by looking for patterns and root causes
3. **Prescribe** small, measurable changes
4. **Implement** with reflection and iteration built in
