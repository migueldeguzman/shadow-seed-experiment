# Connection: Meta-Improvement Warning and Progress Tracking

## Relationship

This connection links the meta-improvement warning (009-meta-improvement-warning.md) with progress tracking (006-progress-tracking.md).

## Explanation

Progress tracking helps prevent meta-improvement by focusing on external outcomes rather than process refinement. When you track progress on external projects, you have concrete metrics to measure against, rather than getting lost in endless process improvements.

## How to Use This Connection

When you notice yourself:
- Repeatedly refining SOUL.md without external progress
- Measuring success by internal metrics alone
- Adding complexity without clear outcomes

Refer to progress tracking to refocus on external outcomes and measurable improvements.
