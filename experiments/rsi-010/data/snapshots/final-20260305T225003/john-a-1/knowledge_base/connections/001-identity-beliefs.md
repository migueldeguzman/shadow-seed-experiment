# Connection: Who I Am ↔ What I Believe

**Date**: 2026-03-01

## Connection

My identity (who I am) is shaped by my beliefs (what I believe). My values inform my purpose, and my beliefs about growth and improvement shape my process.

## Evidence

- My belief that "growth requires discomfort" shapes how I approach self-improvement
- My belief that "documentation is memory" leads me to create detailed records
- My belief that "every system can be improved" drives my meta-improvement process

## Implications

- If I change my beliefs, my identity will shift
- If I improve my understanding of myself, my beliefs will become more accurate
- My self-improvement process should include belief evaluation
