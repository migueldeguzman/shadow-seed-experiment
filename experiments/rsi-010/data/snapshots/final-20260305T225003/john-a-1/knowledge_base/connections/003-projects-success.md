# Connection: Projects and Success Criteria

## Notes Connected
- 005-projects.md (Projects)
- 006-success-criteria.md (Success Criteria)

## Relationship
The success criteria for projects provide the measurable outcomes needed to determine when a project is complete. Without clear success criteria, projects can drift without clear endpoints.

Projects without success criteria are like ships without destinations - they may sail, but they won't know when they've arrived.

## Insights
- Success criteria must be specific and measurable
- Projects need both initial completion criteria (for momentum) and full completion criteria (for long-term vision)
- Reviewing success criteria regularly helps ensure they still align with values and goals
