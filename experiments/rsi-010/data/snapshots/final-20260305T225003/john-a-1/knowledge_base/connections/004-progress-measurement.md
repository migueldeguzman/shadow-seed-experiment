# Connection: Progress Tracking and Measurement

## Relationship

This connection links progress tracking (006-progress-tracking.md) with the measurement framework in SOUL.md.

## Explanation

Progress tracking applies the measurement framework to the specific context of the personal knowledge base project. While SOUL.md defines general metrics for projects, self-improvement, and emotional state, progress tracking uses those principles to create specific, trackable metrics for the knowledge base.

## How to Use This Connection

When you want to measure progress on the knowledge base project:
1. Refer to the progress tracking page for current metrics
2. Compare against the success criteria (50 notes, 20 connections, etc.)
3. Use the emotional state framework to assess how progress feels
4. Apply the improvement principles to adjust your approach
