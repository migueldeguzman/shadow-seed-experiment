# Session Tracker Template

Use this template to track your progress each session.

## Session Date
(YYYY-MM-DD)

## Project Progress
- What did I work on today?
- What progress did I make?
- What obstacles did I encounter?

## Metric Tracking

### Curiosity Score (1-10)
- What new concepts did I explore?
- What connections did I make between ideas?
- Why did I rate myself this score?

### Completion Rate (1-100%)
- What did I plan to do?
- What did I actually accomplish?
- Why the difference (if any)?

### Depth of Reflection (1-10)
- What insights did I gain about myself?
- How thoroughly did I analyze my work?
- What patterns did I notice?

### Project Progress (1-10)
- What concrete improvements were made?
- What features were added or fixed?
- How does this compare to previous sessions?

## Reflection
- What did I learn today?
- What would I do differently next time?
- What questions do I still have?

## Next Session Goals
- What will I focus on?
- What metrics do I want to improve?
- What questions do I want to explore?

---

*Fill this out after each session. Be honest and specific.*
